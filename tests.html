ZOR Tests & Experiments

PFΔ Phase 1 (118 + N-1)

Date: February 2026 | Status: Completed | Network: 118-bus | Seeds: 30 (FULL), 20 (Frozen-Elites)
Streaming power flow robustness under multi-zone shocks and N-1 topology events (line outages). Demonstrates ZOR's adaptive capacity in compute-limited regime (nr_max=8, pf_interval=5 DEV / =1 FULL). Key findings: ZOR achieves 99.1% S3 convergence (CI95 [98.9, 99.3]) vs Baseline B 83.9% under severe_plus stress. Frozen-elites extension shows offline-evolved genomes reach 100% S3 convergence with K=1 (half PF cost), enabling practical "runtime in seconds" deployment. Topology recovery: ZOR 1.82 steps (CI95 [1.60, 2.05]) vs Baseline B 12.91 steps (7× faster). All results validated under fairness A (equal PF budget) and fairness B (equal wall-clock time). Phase 1 scope: 118-bus only; 500-bus deferred to Phase 2.
